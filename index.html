Meet Our Founder: Nando Breure

The Vision Behind SafetyXAcademy

Nando Breure is the visionary founder of SafetyXAcademy, bringing together decades of experience in the safety and maritime industry. With a passion for creating safer working environments and connecting professionals across the globe, Nando established SafetyXAcademy to bridge the gap between quality safety training and meaningful employment opportunities.

His vision is clear: to create an international ecosystem where safety professionals can access world-class training, achieve recognized certifications like NEBOSH, and connect with employers who value their expertise. Through SafetyXAcademy, Nando is building a community that prioritizes safety, knowledge, and professional growth.
