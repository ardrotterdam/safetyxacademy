Iconic landmarks within walking distance

1. Erasmusbrug: the icon of Rotterdam

The Erasmusbrug isn't just the symbol of Rotterdam — it's literally 100 metres from our training centre. This architectural gem connects the northern and southern parts of the city and offers a breathtaking view over the Maas river.

Student tip: the Erasmusbrug walk

Take a walk across the Erasmusbrug at lunchtime. The fresh air and the view do wonders to clear your head after an intensive NEBOSH theory session. Many of our students use this as an "active break" to absorb the material better. From the bridge you can see the whole skyline: from the Zalmhaventoren to the Maastoren.

Practical info Erasmusbrug

  • Distance from Westplein 12: 100 metres (1-minute walk)
  • Best time: morning for a quiet view, evening for the sunset
  • Photo spot: from the water at Veerhaven or in the middle of the bridge
  • Parking: Erasmusbrug car park (5-minute walk)

2. Zalmhaventoren: tallest building in the Benelux

At 215 metres, the Zalmhaventoren is the tallest residential building in the Benelux. Iconic, and perfectly visible from our location.

3. Euromast: Rotterdam's viewpoint

The Euromast is a 10-minute walk from our training centre and offers the best view over Rotterdam from 185 metres up. The perfect spot to celebrate passing your exam.

Euromast: what can you do there?

  • Observation deck: 360° view over Rotterdam
  • Restaurant: Brasserie Euromast
  • Abseiling: 100-metre abseil
  • Distance: 10-minute walk via Parkhaven

NEBOSH success tip

Many of our students celebrate passing their NEBOSH exam with a visit to the Euromast — a great way to round off your journey.

Parking near Westplein 12

Best options:

1. Erasmusbrug car park (recommended)

  • Distance: 5-minute walk
  • Capacity: large, almost always space
  • Address: Wilhelminakade (under the Erasmusbrug)

2. Q-Park Veerhaven

  • Distance: 3-minute walk
  • Address: Veerhaven 1

3. On-street parking

Paid on-street parking is possible but often full. We recommend the car parks.

Getting there by public transport

By far the easiest option for most students.

From Rotterdam Centraal

  • Tram 4 or 23 → stop Parklaan (±5 min)
  • Or a 10-minute walk via Schiedamsesingel → Veerhaven → Westplein

From other cities

  • Amsterdam → Rotterdam CS: ±40 min
  • The Hague → Rotterdam CS: ±20 min
  • Antwerp → Rotterdam CS: ±1 hour
  • Brussels → Rotterdam CS: ±1h15 (direct intercity)

Pro tip: download the 9292 app for door-to-door public transport. Address: Westplein 12, 3016 BM Rotterdam.
