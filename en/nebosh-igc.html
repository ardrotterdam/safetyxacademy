Globally recognised alternative to MVK

Safety X Academy works with an official NEBOSH partner. Through this partner you can obtain the NEBOSH International Certificate — an internationally recognised alternative to the Dutch MVK qualification.

The course is online and flexible, ideal for professionals in ports, offshore, construction and energy.

Benefits

  • Official NEBOSH certificate
  • Online course (flexible study)
  • Affordable: €995 including exam
  • Start whenever you want — no fixed start date
  • Dutch-speaking contact point
  • Please note: the course and exam are in English
