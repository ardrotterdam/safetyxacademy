Why NEBOSH IGC?

The qualification that opens international doors

The NEBOSH International General Certificate (IGC) is the most widely requested entry-level qualification for HSE professionals worldwide. It is the global benchmark for occupational health and safety — recognised in 120+ countries.

Whether you want to work in European ports, offshore, the Middle East or international projects: with NEBOSH IGC on your CV you go further than with a national certificate alone.

  • Globally recognised by employers in ports, offshore, industry and construction
  • Higher day rates as an HSE contractor (€500–€1,500/day depending on region)
  • Open-book exam (IG1) + practical assignment (IG2) — no travel abroad required
  • Serious but achievable: around 8–10 hours of study per week alongside work
  • Direct access to international HSE roles and contract opportunities
Audience

Who is this course for?

The NEBOSH IGC course in Rotterdam is designed for anyone building an international HSE career — with or without a prior HSE qualification.

HSE & safety professionals

You already work in safety and want to become internationally mobile — in ports, offshore or international projects.

Supervisors & team leaders

You lead teams in industry, construction, terminals or offshore and want to formalise your HSE knowledge with a recognised qualification.

HSE contractors & freelancers

You work as a contractor or want to. NEBOSH IGC strengthens your profile and opens doors to better-paid assignments.

Career changers

You have technical experience and want to move into an HSE role — locally or internationally.

Offshore & maritime professionals

You work or want to work in offshore wind, oil & gas or maritime sectors where NEBOSH is often a hard requirement.

Expats & international professionals

You are based in the Netherlands, elsewhere in Europe, or relocating — and want an accessible route to a globally recognised HSE qualification.

Official quality

SafetyXAcademy works with an official NEBOSH partner

NEBOSH does not deliver classroom courses itself — you study through an accredited learning partner. SafetyXAcademy works with an official NEBOSH partner to ensure that you:

  • Follow the official NEBOSH syllabus and learning outcomes
  • Can sit the exam through the recognised NEBOSH procedure
  • Receive a genuine NEBOSH International General Certificate upon successful completion

You get the best of both worlds: local, English-taught guidance in Rotterdam and the international standard that employers worldwide recognise.

Why this matters

  • Only accredited partners can deliver official NEBOSH exams
  • Your certificate is internationally recognised and verifiable
  • You learn to current NEBOSH standards
  • No risk of unrecognised certificates or dubious providers
