NEBOSH Offshore – Work as an HSE professional in Dubai, Norway & the UK

Want to truly earn well as an HSE professional? With a NEBOSH IGC certificate from the Netherlands you can work offshore in Dubai, Norway and the UK with day rates around €1,000–€1,500 per day as a contractor — far higher than most onshore HSE roles.

Why NEBOSH for offshore work?

Offshore employers in Dubai, Norway, the North Sea and the UK increasingly ask for a NEBOSH IGC certificate as the minimum for HSE roles. With only a national qualification you often won't even pass the first screening — with NEBOSH an international market literally opens up for you.

  • Recognised in 120+ countries
  • Higher contractor day rate: often €1,000–€1,500/day offshore
  • Suitable for offshore wind, oil & gas and maritime projects
  • English-taught course in Rotterdam — English exam

Our NEBOSH IGC course in the Netherlands costs €3,500 all-in and is built for international HSE professionals and expats who want to work offshore or internationally.

Where are the best offshore opportunities?

Dubai & the Middle East (UAE, Saudi, Qatar)

In Dubai and the wider Middle East, NEBOSH IGC and offshore experience are often hard requirements for HSE roles. The combination of large projects, tax-free salaries and extra benefits (housing, flights) makes this one of the most attractive regions for ambitious HSE professionals.

  • Typical contractor day rate: ± €1,300/day (indicative)
  • Projects: Jebel Ali, ADNOC, offshore platforms, terminals
  • Many international teams — NEBOSH is the common standard

Norway & the North Sea

In Norway and on the North Sea you'll find a mix of traditional oil & gas and modern offshore wind. With NEBOSH IGC you increase your chances of well-paid HSE roles on platforms and vessels.

  • Indicative day rate: ± €1,200/day
  • Projects: Equinor, Aker BP, offshore wind farms
  • Strong focus on safety, procedures and compliance — NEBOSH fits perfectly

UK & North Sea (Aberdeen, North Sea hubs)

The UK has historically been one of the most important offshore markets in the world. Many companies already work to NEBOSH standards. With an IGC certificate you have a clear advantage when applying.

  • Indicative day rate: ± €1,100/day
  • Projects: BP, Shell, North Sea projects
  • High demand for experienced HSE professionals with NEBOSH + offshore certificates

The Netherlands & European ports: a great starting point, lower rates

In Rotterdam, Antwerp and other European ports, rates are generally lower than on international offshore projects, but it's an excellent place to build experience after your NEBOSH course.

  • Indicative day rate: often €400–€800/day as an HSE contractor (depending on experience)
  • Ideal as a springboard to offshore or the Middle East
  • Be based in Europe and build your HSE track record

From this base you can then make a targeted move into offshore or abroad.

What can you roughly earn offshore with NEBOSH?

Exact rates depend on your experience, project type and region. But based on market information, the rough pattern looks like this:

  • European ports: ± €400–€800/day as an HSE contractor
  • Offshore North Sea / Norway: ± €900–€1,200/day
  • Dubai & Middle East: ± €1,000–€1,500/day (often tax-free, sometimes incl. housing and flights)

Work a few months a year offshore and an annual income above €100,000 as an HSE contractor is realistic — provided you have the right certifications and are willing to work abroad (temporarily).

Frequently asked questions about NEBOSH offshore

Is NEBOSH IGC enough to work offshore?

NEBOSH IGC is often the baseline requirement for HSE roles. For offshore you usually also need BOSIET/FOET, a valid medical and sometimes GWO (for wind). We help you with a personal plan.

Can I work offshore with NEBOSH without years of experience?

Yes, but you'll usually start in junior or medior HSE roles or onshore roles in ports and terminals. From there you can progress to offshore once your experience and additional certificates are in place.

In which countries can I work with NEBOSH IGC?

NEBOSH IGC is recognised in more than 120 countries. For offshore there is high demand in Dubai & the Middle East, Norway, the UK and other regions with large energy and wind projects.
