About SafetyXAcademy

Study in Europe, work worldwide. We're building a small, sharp NEBOSH IGC provider — focused on ports, offshore and industry — and we're honest about where we are right now.

Team

Who we are

SafetyXAcademy is an HSE training provider in the heart of the Port of Rotterdam. We help international professionals and expats earn a NEBOSH International General Certificate (IGC) and better understand career opportunities in ports, offshore and international projects.

Our background is in the real world of:

  • port companies (Rotterdam & Antwerp)
  • offshore wind & oil/gas
  • industry and construction

Toolbox talks on the quay, audits on terminals, permits to work on platforms and shutdowns with tight deadlines — that reality shapes how we build the course.

Our mission

Train professionals into internationally deployable HSE specialists — with clear explanations, small groups and transparent pricing. No empty promises about things we don't (yet) deliver.

Our mission & vision

Where we're heading — concrete and without the fluff

🎯

Mission

Offer strong, English-taught NEBOSH IGC courses in Rotterdam — so professionals from the Netherlands, Europe and beyond can work internationally, without unnecessary barriers or hidden costs.

🌍

Vision

Over the coming years, grow into a trusted NEBOSH hub serving Europe and international professionals — start small, safeguard quality, then scale. Rotterdam stays our home base.

💚

Values

Accessibility, quality and honesty. No claims we can't back up — especially now, while we're still in the build-up phase.
