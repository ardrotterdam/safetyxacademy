Why NEBOSH IGC

The global standard for an international HSE career

Local safety qualifications stay local. NEBOSH IGC opens ports, offshore wind and international projects worldwide — with a globally recognised certificate that travels with you.

For €3,500 all-in (exam fees included) you complete the course with us in the Netherlands. The course and guidance are delivered in English, and the exam is in English too — ideal for expats and international professionals based anywhere in Europe.
