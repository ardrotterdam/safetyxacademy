Rotterdam: the gateway to Europe's ports

Our NEBOSH IGC course in Veerhaven Rotterdam sits at the centre of Europe's busiest port region — ideal for international HSE professionals and expats working across the continent.

Rotterdam

Location: Veerhaven, Westplein 12

Travel time: 0 min (on site)

Ports: Maasvlakte, Europoort — Europe's largest port

Antwerp

Travel time: ~1 hour (train)

Ports: Port of Antwerp-Bruges

Rail: Direct connection from Antwerpen-Centraal

Amsterdam

Travel time: ~1 hour (train)

Ports: Port of Amsterdam

Rail: Direct connection from Amsterdam Centraal

Ghent & North Sea Port

Travel time: ~1.5 hours (train)

Ports: North Sea Port (Ghent–Terneuzen)

Rail: Via Brussels or direct to Rotterdam

Zeebrugge

Travel time: ~2 hours (train)

Ports: Port of Zeebrugge

Rail: Via Bruges and Brussels

Hamburg

Travel time: high-speed rail / short flight

Ports: Port of Hamburg — Germany's largest

Note: A popular destination for NEBOSH-qualified HSE professionals

Why NEBOSH for a port-based HSE career?

Central location: Europe's major ports are within easy reach of our training location in Veerhaven Rotterdam. Rail connections from Antwerp, Amsterdam, Ghent and Zeebrugge are excellent, and Hamburg is a short hop away.

Blended learning: You don't have to travel every week. Most self-study is done from home, and the classroom sessions are concentrated into a few days per month — convenient for professionals and expats already working in the sector.

Internationally recognised: With NEBOSH IGC you can work in 120+ countries, including Norway, the UK, Dubai and other international hubs where HSE contracting demand is high.

Higher day rates: After your course you can work as a contractor in the ports where you're already active. Read our HSE jobs & contracting day rates overview for a practical breakdown by experience level.

Cost: €3,500 all-in including exam fees — competitive with other safety qualifications and with far more international opportunities.
